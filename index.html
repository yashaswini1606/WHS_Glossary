A
Away from duty for more than 24 hours but less than 48 Hours
Lagging Performance Indicators. (Excluding GPL & OEM) – An injury causing disablement extending beyond the day of shift on which the accident occurred. (Defined in terminology 2.3 of IS 3786 (1883)).
Away from duty for more than 48 Hours
Lagging Performance Indicators – An accident which causes any bodily injury by reason of which the person injured is prevented from working for a period of forty-eight hours or more immediately following the accident or which is of such nature. (Refer: Section 88 of Factory Act, 1948).
F
First Aid
Lagging Performance Indicators – Treatment of minor bodily injury which does not require treatment by a registered medical practitioner or nurse.
